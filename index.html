Sterka M1:
The Modular DIY Pedelec Bike Kit

Sterka M1 is a premium, powerful and modular out-of-the-box, build-it-yourself e-bike that you can upgrade, customize and build. Inspired by a career in professional RC Racing, father and son, Rick and JJ Wang set out to develop an e-bike kit that would appeal to builders, mountain bikers looking to break into eMTB riding and anyone who has a love to tinker!

Electric Bike Project on Kickstarter

Inspired from over 10 years of RC racing, JJ set out to create a machine encompassing his love for heightened design and incredible performance with Sterka’s 1st model, the M1. A tinkerer and builder himself, he sought to create the same experience found in his RC racing career. Currently a Junior at USC’s Marshall School of Business, he’s taken the lessons he’s learned to get started with Sterka Concepts.
